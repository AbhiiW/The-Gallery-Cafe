Welcome to The Gallerycafe

Located in the vibrant heart of Colombo, The Gallery Café is a celebrated dining spot offering an exceptional culinary experience with a diverse menu that includes Italian, Sri Lankan, Chinese, and Korean cuisines. Our chic and contemporary ambiance, adorned with elegant decor and art pieces, creates the perfect setting for any occasion, whether a romantic dinner, a family gathering, or a business meeting. Our skilled chefs use only the finest ingredients to craft each dish, ensuring a delightful gastronomic journey for our guests. Enjoy the convenience of our online reservation system, secure your preferred dining time and table, and indulge in a memorable dining experience where every meal is a masterpiece. Join us at The Gallery Café and discover why we are a beloved dining destination in Colombo.
